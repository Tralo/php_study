zendFramework配置
1.在php.ini文件中
extension=php_pdo_mysql.dll （去掉分号）

2.开启Apache的rewrite模块，在httpd.conf
#启用 rewrite功能
LoadModule rewrite_module modules/mod_rewrite.so

AllowOverride none 改为all


# Virtual hosts   启动虚拟主机的配置
Include conf/extra/httpd-vhosts.conf   (去掉#)


在httpd-vhosts.conf中
#配置我们自己的虚拟主机
<VirtualHost *:80>
    DocumentRoot "C:/Users/Administrator/Desktop/gitproject/php_study/zend_study/testzf2\library"
    ServerName myzf.com
    DirectoryIndex index.php
	<Directory />
	Options FollowSymLinks
	AllowOverride None
	Order allow,deny
	Allow from all
	</Directory>
</VirtualHost>
